Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Mechanic Since 1990

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep Dallas drivers safe and satisfied.

M & F Automotive Group Inc was founded in 1990 with a simple mission: deliver reliable auto repair with integrity. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small shop into a trusted name in Dallas, serving thousands of customers. Our team of ASE-certified technicians brings years of experience and a commitment to excellence on every vehicle.

We believe in transparency, quality parts, and fair pricing. Whether you need a quick oil change or a major engine repair, we treat your car as if it were our own. Our reputation is built on trust, and we’re proud to be a family-owned business that puts customers first.
